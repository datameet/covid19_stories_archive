Rizvi continues his attack on Tablighi Jamaat

Lucknow, Apr 9 (UNI) Uttar Pradesh Shia Central Waqf Board chairman Wasim Rizvi shot yet another salvo against the Sunni muslim radical organisation Tablighi Jamaat and has alleged that the Jamaat had hatched a conspiracy to launch suicidal attack in India.
He charged that the Tablighi Jamaat had planned to kill over one lakh people in India.
Mr Rizvi, on Thursday in a statement, also attacked Zakir Naik, presently living in hiding in Malaysia. He said there is distinct possibility that Zakir Naik is also involved in the conspiracy. He said the involvement of Zakir Naik cannot be ruled out as large number of Tablighi Jamaat members corona virus positive infection have been identified in Malaysia.
Wasim Rizvi alleged that the misbehaviour and violence against the doctors and para medical staff by the Tablighi Jamaat members sent to quarantine is also part of the well organised conspiracy by the Jamaat. He said the Jamaat by attacking doctors is trying to kill the morale of the medicos.
Mr Rizvi had been relentlessly attacking the Sunni radical Muslims and their organisations.
On Wednesday he had demanded the reparation cost be imposed on the members of the Tablighi Jamaat for spreading the pandemic Corona virus in India. He also demanded that criminal case be registered against Tableegi jamat Chief Maulana Saad for spreading the corona virus in India. Rizvi also demanded that bodies of the victims of the corona virus be cremated irrespective of caste and religion.
Mr Rizvi had also obtained a fatwa from Iran to build a case against Tabllegi jamat. The fatwa was given by 93 year old Ayatollah Nasir Makrim of Iran who has ruled that the person responsible for spreading the disease will have to pay the cost. The fatwa also said if that person dies from that disease then he will be held responsible for that.
Rizvi charged that ``even as the entire India was fighting the menace of the COVID 19, it spread like wild fire due to the religious congregation at the Markaz (headquarter) of the tableegi jamat at Delhi. As per the Shariat law and law of India Maulana Saad head of Tableegi jamaat is solely responsible for spreading the epidemic and entire cost of containing the epidemic be realised from Maulana Saad, said Wasim Rizvi.
