Jharkhand: After losing job due to lockdown, man commits suicide in Bokaro

Bokaro, May 30 (UNI) Deeply stressed after losing his job due to lockdown, a man committed suicide under Tenughat OP of this district.
The deceased Sanjay Pathak (28) had come to his elder brother's home on March 20 but as lockdown was declared he could not return to Giridih where he worked in a plant
The family members said that Sanjay lost his job due to lockdown and was worried about the future of his family and kids.
On Friday night everyone went to sleep after dinner, but found Sanjay hanging inside a room when they woke up today in the morning.
